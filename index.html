Where you are

Oil/tincture troubleshooting

Your sous vide attempt likely underperformed due to one of three issues.

  • Decarb step: raw flower needs ~95-110°C for 60-90 min in the sealed bag before infusing, or THC stays mostly inactive.
  • Fat ratio: THC is fat-soluble — too little fat, or a low-fat carrier oil, reduces extraction.
  • Dose miscalculation: the oil may be working but at a much lower effective dose than 5 cones.

For now, a store-bought CBD-dominant tincture with a known concentration is more useful during the taper than fixing the homemade oil — precise dosing matters more here.

Core sleep anchors

  • Wake time: 7:15am, every day including weekends — this is the primary anchor.
  • Bedtime: midnight or after, occasional later nights allowed (max 1-2/week).
  • Sunlight: get outside within 30 minutes of waking.
  • Day after a late night: sunlight + light exercise, then return to standard schedule — don't sleep in.

Supplements (current)

  • Magnesium glycinate: 300-400mg, ~1hr before bed — supports GABA pathways, helps with irritability/anxiety during taper.
  • L-theanine: 200mg as needed if evenings feel wired — non-sedating anxiolytic.
  • Melatonin (redose substitution): 0.5-1mg only — low doses work better for re-initiating sleep without grogginess or added tolerance.

Redose substitution protocol

When waking at 2-4am, before reaching for cannabis:

  • 0.5-1mg melatonin
  • 4-7-8 breathing or progressive muscle relaxation
  • Keep the room dark — no phone, no clock-watching
  • Only redose with cannabis if still awake after 20+ minutes

Airway trial

  • Nasal tape: trial first, track awakenings, dry mouth, morning grogginess.
  • SnoreMD: compare separately — known issue with slipping due to front tooth anatomy.
  • Goal: work out how much of the awakening pattern is airway-driven vs withdrawal-driven.

What to expect during the taper

  • Sleep onset may stretch to 45-60 min on some nights during Phase 1-2 — expected and temporary.
  • Most cannabis-withdrawal sleep disruption resolves within 4-8 weeks, though some lingering effects can last longer.
  • Vivid dreams and lighter sleep in the early weeks of reduction are common (REM rebound).
  • Irritability and anxiety are common withdrawal symptoms — not dangerous, but worth tracking.

Hold steady on

  • Don't change cannabis dose and bedtime/wake schedule in the same week — isolate variables.
  • Don't panic-redose to "fix" a slow night during the taper — note it and move on.
  • Re-run a short sleep diary block at the end of each phase and compare to baseline.
